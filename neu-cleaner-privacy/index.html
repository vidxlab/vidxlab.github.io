Privacy Policy for Neu Cleaner

Last updated: April 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Vidx Lab ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and protects information from users ("User") of the application Neu Cleaner ("Service").

By using Neu Cleaner, you agree to this Privacy Policy.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide Voluntarily

Neu Cleaner does not require users to provide personal information to use core features.

Information Collected Automatically

  • Log Data & Crash Reports: We use Firebase Crashlytics to collect crash logs, device information, and error reports.
  • Analytics Data: We use Google Analytics for Firebase to understand app usage and improve performance.
  • Advertising ID: Used by Google AdMob to show relevant ads based on user consent.

Storage Access

Neu Cleaner may access device storage to detect and remove junk files, cache, and unnecessary data. This data is processed locally and is not uploaded to our servers.

Contacts Data (if applicable)

If you use features such as duplicate contact cleaning, the app may request access to your contacts. This data is processed locally on your device and is not collected, stored, or shared.

How We Use Information

  • To improve app functionality and performance
  • To fix bugs and crashes
  • To provide cleaning and optimization features
  • To display ads (with user consent)

Third-Party Services

Neu Cleaner uses third-party services which may collect information:

  • Firebase Crashlytics

User Consent (Consent Mode V2)

We respect user privacy choices. Users may grant or deny consent for analytics and ads. You can change preferences anytime within the app.

Cookies

We do not use cookies directly. However, third-party services may use similar technologies for analytics and advertising.

Security

We take reasonable steps to protect user data, but no method is 100% secure.

Children’s Privacy

Neu Cleaner is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.
